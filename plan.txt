### PRIMITIVE DATE COMMAND CLONE, 
### WITH A FEATURE TO DISPLAY DAYS LEFT TO JAN 1ST 2030 00:00

Features:
	1. Display date
	2. pass in the -a --agenda option to get the days left to January 1st 2030 00:00
	
Backend Features:
	1. Fetch date with a syscall.
	2. Get the seconds remaining to January 1st 2030 00:00
	3. Error handling
	4. A message that gets displayed after January 1st 2030 00:00
